Incident Management SOP

Priority 1 incidents affect all customers or critical revenue systems. The response SLA is 15
minutes, and the incident commander must open a bridge within 10 minutes. Status updates must be
posted every 30 minutes until mitigation.

Priority 2 incidents affect a subset of customers or important internal workflows. The response
SLA is 1 hour, and updates are required every 2 hours.

After resolution, the owner must publish a post-incident review within 3 business days.
